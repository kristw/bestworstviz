Making terrible charts great again! #BestWorstViz

Each mark in this visualization represents a visualization from viz.wtf, a well-known guide to best practice in data visualization. These 100 visualizations are carefully collected, i.e., scroll down and enter data into a spreadsheet until I reach the 100th. The variables are popularity (number of notes), position of the visualization on the page, main color, visualization type and whether it uses 3D.

You can clearly see the emerging pattern from this carefully encoded visualization, that is nothing can be interpreted without blowing your mind first. Let's see if you can figure out everything that is wrong with this chart. Raw data are also available (data.pdf).
